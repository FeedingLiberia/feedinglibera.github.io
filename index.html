Agriculture

FELA runs a 4-acre farming collective in Grand Gedeh County where it is testing its farming techniques.

Education

FELA leads Scholarship Fund Drives for local students in Monrovia. As the collective grows, we look to bring students onto our farm to learn about agriculture!

Health

FELA seeks to end hunger in Liberia, thus preventing malnutrition and starvation.

Human Rights

FELA fights for the rights of all, particularly children.

The FELA Feeding Program

FELA is committed to feeding the poor (including students and street dwellers). We offer them cooked and raw food both, including meat.

Liberia will be a better nation because of us. Join us!
